NORTHVIEW HEIGHTS SECONDARY SCHOOL

Northview Heights Secondary School is located in the city of Toronto. The school has a long and strong history of community service. It is a multicultural school with about 1500 students during the day.

Northview Heights SS's team is dedicated to delivering outstanding academic and technology programs that challenge our student population in a safe and caring atmosphere.
